About Me

Image

Noura Khaki is a second-year Graphic design major student at OCAD University in Toronto Canada. She has a Visual arts diploma from Alexander Mackenzie highschool. Her background is in design, painting, filmmaking, and photography. Noura is interested in her field of study and art in general. She is always interested to learn and discovering the world around her in other to be able to understand the world better. Her hunger for knowledge and determination about learning turns her into a designer who tries so many elements in her works and designs. Noura believes in order to be successful you need to find a way to love what you are doing, and when you find it you should find yourself very lucky.
